Privacy Policy - Beacon of New Beginnings

Your Safety and Privacy Are Our Top Priority
Beacon of New Beginnings is designed specifically for survivors of abuse and homelessness. We understand that privacy and anonymity are critical for your safety.

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Anonymous Usage

Our app is designed to be used anonymously. You can access most features without providing any personal information.

Optional Information

  • Location Data: Only collected if you choose to find nearby resources
  • Community Posts: Anonymous forum posts (no personal identifiers)
  • Support Contacts: Only if you choose to contact external services

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Names, addresses, or identifying information
  • Phone numbers or email addresses (unless voluntarily provided)
  • Social security numbers or government IDs
  • Financial information
  • Tracking data or behavioral analytics

2. How We Use Information

Safety-First Approach

  • Provide crisis resources and emergency contacts
  • Connect you with local support services
  • Facilitate anonymous community support
  • Improve app functionality and safety features

We Do NOT Use Information To

  • Track or monitor users
  • Share with third parties for marketing
  • Create user profiles or behavioral analytics
  • Generate revenue through data sales

3. Data Security

Security Measures:
  • All data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • No personal data stored unless absolutely necessary
  • Secure communication channels
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Quick exit feature for emergency situations

4. Data Sharing

We Do NOT Share Data With

  • Marketing companies
  • Data brokers
  • Social media platforms
  • Government agencies (except as required by law)

Limited Sharing for Safety

We may share information only when:

  • You explicitly request connection to external support services
  • Required by law to prevent imminent harm
  • Necessary for emergency medical assistance

5. Your Rights and Choices

Anonymous Access

  • Use the app without creating an account
  • Access crisis resources without identification
  • Participate in community support anonymously

Data Control

  • Delete any data you've provided
  • Opt out of location services
  • Clear app data at any time
  • Request data deletion by contacting support

6. Crisis Resources and External Services

When you contact external crisis resources or support services through our app:

  • Their privacy policies apply
  • We don't control their data practices
  • Connection is voluntary and user-initiated
  • We recommend reviewing their privacy policies

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for adults (18+) and does not knowingly collect information from minors. If you're under 18 and need crisis support, please contact:

  • National Child Abuse Hotline: 1-800-4-A-CHILD
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • Local emergency services: 911

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of significant changes through the app.

9. International Users

This app is primarily designed for users in the United States. If you're using it from another country, please be aware that data may be transmitted to and stored in the United States.
