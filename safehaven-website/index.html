About Beacon of New Beginnings

Building a Ghana where every person has access to safety, justice, and dignity

Our Mission

To provide safety, healing, and empowerment to survivors of abuse and homelessness through comprehensive shelter services, professional counseling, legal advocacy, and sustainable livelihood programs that restore dignity and independence.

Our Vision

A Ghana where every person, regardless of their past trauma or circumstances, has guaranteed access to safety, justice, and the resources needed to build a dignified, independent life free from violence and poverty.

Our Values

  • Safety First: Your physical and emotional safety is our top priority
  • Dignity & Respect: Every person deserves to be treated with honor and compassion
  • Empowerment: We help you build the skills and confidence to thrive independently
  • Confidentiality: Your privacy and story are protected at all times
  • Cultural Sensitivity: We honor Ghanaian values while promoting equality
